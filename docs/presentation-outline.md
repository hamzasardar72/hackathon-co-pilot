# ClaimWise AI Presentation Outline

## 1. Problem
Insurance claims have slow manual review steps, inconsistent damage assessment, and high operational cost.

## 2. Solution
ClaimWise AI combines multimodal claim intake, explainable AI, and human-in-the-loop decisioning for faster claim triage.

## 3. Demo Flow
- Submit claim details
- Upload image and PDF
- Run AI analysis
- Review recommendation
- Approve/reject/modify
- Export PDF report

## 4. Business Impact
- Faster turnaround time
- Lower review overhead
- Improved fraud detection
- Better auditability
